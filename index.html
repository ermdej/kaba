About Kaba Coffee

Kaba coffee roasting is established in 2023, one of the specialty coffee roaster based in Addis Ababa. Our mission is to serve a quality Ethiopian coffee for peoples across the world. We produce roasted coffee with different packaging sizes and we deliver roasted coffee for coffee shops, supermarkets, foreign markets, restaurants & cafes.

Coffee

Our Roasting Levels

Light Roast

Typically stay true to their original flavors. You will be able to taste full body, some floral notes, bright wine acidity.

Medium Roast

This roast level tend to bring to mind other flavors like caramel, honey, or roasted fruits.

Medium Dark Roast

This tastes chocolatey darkness, and a bit of floral, berry, fruit, citrus.

Origins Of Our Premium Coffee Beans

Sidamo

Best known for its rich mouthfeels,low acidity, with a vibrant aftertaste, full bodies, and sweet and complex flavor profiles. It is one of the best-known flavor in the world.

Yirgacheffee

It has a sweet and fruity flavor and aroma with a light to medium and rich body, fine acidity.

Harar

bright in the cup with winey and fruity, floral-toned acidity with a rich & pungent taste, heady aroma that is reminiscent of blackberries.
